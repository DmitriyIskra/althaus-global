ALTHAUS offers contemporary concepts for distinguished gastronomy: The highest product quality and modern production methods combined with surprising tea compositions and an innovative design concept.

Hotel

In tune with the contemporary demands of sophisticated catering and fine hotels, ALTHAUS has developed a concept that sets new standards of quality and esthetic appeal.

Pure enjoyment of tea relies on the interaction of many factors. We pay attention to every single one. During regular visits to preferred growing areas, we have established thriving relationships with the best tea plantations. Our trusted partners import the teas, which were carefully selected by us. Afterwards, all refining processes are carried out in Germany under our strict supervision. Also, the final decision is made by our experts. Without their quality approval, no tea leaves our house.

Lobby

From its wide collection of premium teas Althaus has allotted a mix of hot and iced teas, alcohol and alcohol-free tea cocktails, to be served as a refreshment drink or to accompany a set of light snacks and desserts in a lobby bar. If you are planning to set up a self-service-station to cater to waiting guests, Althaus will not only supply recommendations for serving concepts, but the well thought-out equipment and accessories to furnish it.

Loose tea

Available in 70 different blends

  • Black tea
  • Green tea
  • Fruit infusion
  • Herbal infusion
  • Loose tea in its purest form
  • 65g to 250g bags

The more than 70 blends of loose tea are best served “à la carte”. In their purest form, the carefully selected teas develop their full aroma – be it in a strainer or paper filter. For tea enthusiasts, allowing the leaves to move around freely and unfold is an absolute must.

Grand pack

Available in 16 different blends

  • Black tea
  • Green tea
  • Fruit infusion
  • Herbal infusion
  • Preportioned loose tea

Breakfast and Buffet

A solid breakfast is essential to get a good start of the day. Your guests will be pleased to start the morning with an assortment of their favorite flavors. Choose from a range of presentation displays for a clear overview of the selection on a buffet or present individual tea menus for a proper service, individually and easily brewed with Deli Pack, Pyra Pack or even loose tea.

Pyra pack

Available in 15 different blends

  • Black tea
  • Green tea
  • Fruit infusion
  • Herbal infusion
  • Innovative pyramid bag
  • Ideal for mug portions (0.3 l)

The Pyra Pack presents a modern and innovative way of enjoying tea. Thanks to the pyramid-shape tea bag, loose tea leaves are provided with plenty of room to develop their full aroma and flavor. Watching the leaves unfold in the transparent bag is part of the ceremony for every tea appreciator.
