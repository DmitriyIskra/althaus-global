ALTHAUS offers contemporary concepts for distinguished gastronomy: The highest product quality and modern production methods combined with surprising tea compositions and an innovative design concept.

Conferences & Office

Offering hot drinks and refreshments during conference breaks is always in demand for a hotel catering service. Althaus offers a selection of teas in Deli or Pyra packaging, as well as the full set of accessories and porcelain. We know that the most important part of a successful tea break is the quick and easy service with a clear layout for large groups of participants that are thoroughly immerged in their topics and conversations.

Deli pack

Available in 14 different blends

  • Black tea
  • Green tea
  • Fruit infusion
  • Herbal infusion
  • Bags are individually sealed
  • Ideal for cup portions (0.2 l)

Deli Pack — the classic tea bag — offers the perfect preportioned option for a convenient and quick cup of tea. The premium tea is available in many different blends and offers a delicious variety of classics and exciting new compositions. With its short infusion time, drinking Deli Pack tea is always a convenient pleasure.

Go to Store

Pyra pack

Available in 15 different blends

  • Black tea
  • Green tea
  • Fruit infusion
  • Herbal infusion
  • Innovative pyramid bag
  • Ideal for mug portions (0.3 l)

The Pyra Pack presents a modern and innovative way of enjoying tea. Thanks to the pyramid-shape tea bag, loose tea leaves are provided with plenty of room to develop their full aroma and flavor. Watching the leaves unfold in the transparent bag is part of the ceremony for every tea appreciator.
